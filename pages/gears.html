My Gears

List of stuff that i use everyday. I also attached Tokopedia link to the product.


Phone

The old ASUS Zenfone 2 Laser does the job. I don't need a high-end phone because i don't do gaming on my phone. 1GiB RAM, Mediatek CPU, and 8GiB internal memory, this thing is enough.
ASUS Zenfone 2 Laser ZE500KL


Watch

I wear an SKMEI 1213. A cheap low-tier watch that you can even buy this thing now. Despite that, this watch is durable, waterproof, does the job, and easy to set. It got complete basic features like 12/24hours mode, backlight, alarm, stopwatch and countdown. And also i like the simple look of it. It looks like the famous Suunto Traverse watch.
SKMEI 1213


Powerbank

UNEED UPBL-8.1. It's cheap, high-capacity, and durable. I bought this thing like a year ago and it lasts untill now without any problem (except the USB cable). And also got nice thin design.
UNEED UPBL-8.1


Earphones

SONY MDR EX15AP. This thing got high-resolution treble and mid range with powerful bass. It's comfortable to wear and sounds really good. And also got cord slider and microphone with a single button that allows you to answer calls, pause and play music, and go to the next song by clicking it twice. That button is also programmable with an app. It came with 3 different size of eartips.
SONY MDR EX15AP
